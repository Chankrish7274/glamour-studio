Our Legacy

Crafting Fine Southern Flavors

At Spice Garden, we pay homage to the rich regional history of South Indian culinary traditions. Our chefs source heirloom varieties of spices directly from the hills of Coorg and Chettinad, grinding them daily to release the oils that give our curries and roast plates their legendary depth.

"To build an immersive dining experience where centuries-old recipes, modern luxury presentation, and absolute purity meet."

From the coastal seafood delicacies of Malabar to the fiery roasted spice profiles of Tamil Nadu, each dish in our menu is a celebration of origin, culture, and high-quality local produce.
